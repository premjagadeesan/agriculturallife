Marketing Tips

Selling Tips:



• Collect the full payment before or at the time of the delivery. Use a safe, open location to meet

• Do not accept partial payment/payment in instalments

• Double check the buyer details, since it is impossible identify a fraudulent buyer

• Get a verifiable telephone number from the buyer. If possible get an alternate telephone number or email id of the buyer

• Ensure that your product meets the features you list in your ad, to avoid misunderstanding

• Do not share your financial details unnecessarily with anyone

Purchasing Tips:



• Try to meet the seller directly, check the quality and quantity of the product before making payments

• Check all information on the condition of the goods, price, payment and delivery methods beforehand

• Use only those secure, trustworthy and foolproof payment methods

• Ask for alternate contact details of the seller

• While ordering fruits and vegetables, ask about the condition of the products when delivered

• Check the market price for a product against the price quoted

• Do not share your financial details unnecessarily with anyone

• If the product is shipped, get reliable tracking information
